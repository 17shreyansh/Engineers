# Industrial B2B Theme Update - Complete

## Overview
Successfully transformed the website from a modern startup aesthetic to a traditional industrial B2B engineering brand identity.

## Theme Philosophy
**"We've been doing this for years" energy**
- Reliability over creativity
- Experience over innovation  
- Functionality over aesthetics
- Trust-first approach

## Color Scheme Changes

### Primary Colors
- **Charcoal Black**: `#1A1A1A` (primary), `#2B2B2B` (secondary)
  - Authority, seriousness, no-nonsense
  
- **Industrial Green**: `#A6B43A` (accent), `#9FB02A` (light), `#8A9B25` (dark)
  - Cold storage → freshness
  - Insulation → efficiency
  - Engineering → safety
  - Muted, mature, operational reliability

- **Gold/Mustard**: `#C99A2E`, `#D4A017`
  - Legacy and authority
  - Distributor credibility
  - "Official" status markers
  - Used for badges, subtitles, accents

- **Off-White Background**: `#F5F3EF`
  - Clean, neutral, trust
  - Paper/document feel
  - Base trust layer

- **Rust Red**: `#9B3A2E`
  - Traditional Indian business tone
  - Manufacturing heritage

## Typography Changes

### Fonts
- **Headings**: Roboto Slab (serif) - Traditional, authoritative
- **Body**: Roboto (sans-serif) - Clean, readable, professional

### Font Weights
- Increased to 700-900 for headings (bold, heavy)
- Body text at 400 (normal weight)
- Letter spacing increased to 0.08-0.1em for uppercase text

## Design Language Updates

### Layout Style
- More vertical, certificate-like layouts
- Symmetrical and center-aligned
- Print-oriented feel (not digital-native)
- Conservative spacing

### Borders & Shadows
- Changed from 1px to 2px borders (stronger, more defined)
- Reduced shadow intensity (from xl to sm/md)
- Solid borders instead of subtle ones
- Less "floating" effect, more grounded

### Border Radius
- Reduced from lg/xl to sm (less rounded, more formal)
- More rectangular, less modern curves

### Animations & Transitions
- Reduced transform distances (8px → 4px, 4px → 2px)
- Slower, more conservative hover effects
- Removed rotation effects
- Less "playful" interactions

### Buttons
- Solid backgrounds instead of gradients
- 2px borders for definition
- Gold border on hover for authority
- Uppercase with increased letter-spacing
- Smaller border radius (sm instead of md)

### Cards
- 2px solid borders (stronger definition)
- Top/bottom accent bars instead of side bars
- Reduced hover lift (4px instead of 8px)
- Solid colors instead of gradients
- More rectangular shapes

## Component-Specific Changes
